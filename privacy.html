Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

概述

BizSteps（以下简称「本应用」）尊重并保护用户隐私。本隐私政策说明了本应用如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

我们收集的信息

本应用不会收集任何个人身份信息，也不会向任何第三方服务器传输用户数据。

您在应用内创建的所有数据（业务流程、联系人、文档、执行记录、照片）均仅保存在以下位置：

  • 本机存储：SwiftData 本地数据库，仅存在于您的设备上。
  • iCloud 私有数据库（可选）：若您选择开启同步，数据将通过您的 Apple ID 对应的 iCloud 私有数据库进行加密传输，仅您本人可访问，开发者无法读取。

我们不做的事

  • 不收集任何崩溃日志或诊断数据。
  • 不接入任何第三方分析 SDK（如 Firebase、Mixpanel 等）。
  • 不出售或共享您的任何数据。
  • 不在应用内展示广告。

照片权限

执行流程时，应用会请求访问您的相册以完成「拍照采集」步骤。照片仅保存在本机数据库中，不会上传至任何服务器。

通知权限

若您设置了业务流提醒，应用会请求通知权限，用于在指定时间发送本地通知。通知数据不经过任何外部服务器。

儿童隐私

本应用面向成年商业用户，不面向 13 岁以下儿童。

政策变更

如本政策有重大变更，我们将在应用更新说明中进行通知。

联系我们

如有隐私相关疑问，请联系：support@bizsteps.app

Overview

BizSteps ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information and does not transmit user data to any third-party servers.

All data you create within the App (workflows, contacts, documents, execution history, photos) is stored only in the following locations:

  • On-device storage: A local SwiftData database that exists only on your device.
  • iCloud Private Database (optional): If you enable sync, data is encrypted and transmitted through your personal iCloud private database, accessible only by you. Developers cannot access this data.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect crash logs or diagnostic data.
  • We do not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel).
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone.
  • We do not show advertisements.

Photo Library Access

During workflow execution, the App may request access to your photo library for "photo capture" steps. Photos are stored only in the local on-device database and are never uploaded to any server.

Notifications

If you set workflow reminders, the App will request notification permission to deliver local notifications at the scheduled time. No notification data passes through any external server.

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for adult business users and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you in the App's update release notes.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact: support@bizsteps.app
