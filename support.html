常见问题

如何在 iPhone 上执行流程？

在 Mac 端的「画布」中创建并发布业务流后，打开 iPhone 端 BizSteps，点击对应业务流，即可按步骤执行。

iCloud 同步不生效怎么办？

请确认：① 已在设置中登录 iCloud 账号；② 两台设备使用同一 Apple ID；③ 系统「设置 → Apple 账户 → iCloud」中已开启 BizSteps 数据同步。

照片数据存储在哪里？

执行流程时拍摄的照片仅保存在本机 SwiftData 数据库中，不会上传至任何服务器。iCloud 同步功能同步的是流程结构数据，不包含照片。

如何更改应用语言？

打开 BizSteps → 设置 → 语言，选择简体中文、繁体中文、English 或 日本語。

支持哪些 iOS 版本？

需要 iOS 17.0 或更高版本，以及 macOS 14.0 或更高版本。

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I execute a workflow on iPhone?

After creating and publishing a workflow in the Mac Canvas, open BizSteps on iPhone and tap the workflow to start step-by-step execution.

iCloud sync isn't working — what should I do?

Please check: ① You're signed into iCloud on both devices; ② Both devices use the same Apple ID; ③ BizSteps is enabled under Settings → Apple Account → iCloud.

Where are my photos stored?

Photos captured during execution are stored only in the local SwiftData database on your device. They are not uploaded to any server. iCloud sync covers workflow structure data only, not photos.

How do I change the app language?

Open BizSteps → Settings → Language, then choose from Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, or Japanese.

What iOS version is required?

iOS 17.0 or later is required. macOS 14.0 or later for the Mac app.
